About A.P.E

Automatic Presentation Evaluation (A.P.E) is a revolutionary solution for smart education assessment. At A.P.E, we're a team of dedicated individuals passionate about enhancing the educational experience through cutting-edge technology. Our mission is to streamline the evaluation process for student presentations, empowering educators and students alike. By harnessing the power of machine learning algorithms, we've developed a comprehensive system that assesses various aspects of a presentation, including confidence, expression, and sentiment analysis.

Our Automatic Presentation Evaluation system is designed to empower students, professionals, and anyone looking to enhance their presentation skills. By harnessing the power of facial recognition, audio analysis, and sentiment interpretation, our platform offers transformative feedback that drives growth and proficiency in public speaking and communication.

Our Automatic Presentation Evaluation system leverages cutting-edge technology to provide comprehensive feedback on presentation skills. By analyzing facial expressions, speech delivery, and sentiment, our application offers valuable insights that empower students to enhance their communication prowess.

Functions

  • Facial Emotion Analysis:

    Utilizing sophisticated facial recognition algorithms, our system accurately identifies emotions displayed during a presentation. Emotions such as happiness, surprise, sadness, and more are assessed to gauge the presenter's engagement and expressiveness.

  • Speech Delivery Analysis:

    Our platform analyzes the audio components of a presentation, evaluating critical factors like speech rate, pitch, and amplitude. This analysis provides detailed feedback on the pace, clarity, and modulation of the presenter's voice.

  • Sentiment Analysis:

    Leveraging sentiment analysis techniques, our system interprets the overall sentiment conveyed in the presentation. By understanding the emotional tone of the presentation, our tool helps students refine their messaging and connect more effectively with their audience.

  • Combination of Metrics:

    Our unique approach combines multiple metrics—facial emotions, speech characteristics, and sentiment—to generate a holistic evaluation. This comprehensive analysis provides a nuanced understanding of a presenter's strengths and areas for improvement.
